Platforms need to Balance the Rights of Speakers and Listeners

If either one of the two is given preferential treatment, the other suffers! How do we find an equilibrium? Audiences provide platforms with reach Platforms should be accountable to protect audiences. Free speech is an absolutely vital + legal right. But we need mechanisms to introduce accountability in exchange for reach!
